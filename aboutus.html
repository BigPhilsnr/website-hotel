About

Since 1997, Royale Health Club has offered visitors to Nairobi an excellent standard of accommodation, a charming portfolio of unique rooms to choose from, and exceptional value for money. And we've remained an independent family business all that time, so a warm welcome and award-winning attention to detail is guaranteed. Room rates are all-inclusive, with no hidden extras, but our standards are maintained without compromise. And our dedication to value is not confined to our hotel rooms: every one of our remarkable portfolio of bars and restaurant, is priced with the same principle in mind..
